Salad Prices in JavaScript

Have you ever wanted to eat healthy, but you didn't know how much money salads are? Well you're sort of in luck, as this website will help determine the price of a salad that is customized by you.

There is no set in stone price for a salad meaning this is entirely based on the average salad price. You should not be expecting the exact price when going to your local salad-store. This will give you an idea on how much money should be brought. (in Canadian currency)

You have the option to purchase toppings for your salad. The options are croutons ($0.50), shredded cheese ($0.50), salad dressing ($0.50), and fruits ($0.50)
